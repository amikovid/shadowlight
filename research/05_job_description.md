## Chief of Staff

**Location:** Remote / Thailand-based listing (remote-friendly, bonus if you overlap with LA / HK / Australia hours)
**Type:** Full-time
**Posted:** 14 days ago

## Founder's Associate / Chief of Staff (aka "Chief Achiever")

Work directly with a serial founder across a portfolio of fast-moving companies.

## The Gig

I'm a serial founder currently running or building across a handful of companies; an AI language-learning app (Volley), an influencer verification platform, a YouTube growth agency, a global hiring platform, and a couple more. Previously built and exited an agency to $20M ARR.

I need someone smart, scrappy, and AI-native sitting next to me (virtually) every day, helping me move these businesses forward. Not a project manager. Not an assistant. A doer who can pick up almost anything I throw at them, figure it out, and ship it.

If you're the kind of person who sees a problem, opens Cursor or Lovable, watches a YouTube tutorial, and has something working by end of day, we should talk.

## What you'll actually do (examples from a real week):

- **Monday:** Launch and optimize Meta ad campaigns for Volley. Test 5 new creative angles, kill the losers, scale the winners.
- **Tuesday:** Vibe-code a new onboarding screen in Cursor or spin up a landing page variant in Lovable for A/B testing.
- **Wednesday:** Source and cold-outreach 20 senior sales hires on LinkedIn for a portfolio company. Draft the messages yourself.
- **Thursday:** Audit data quality on Verify Influencers, spot-check 100 profiles, flag inaccuracies, write a loom for the team.
- **Friday:** Review a sales call recording, pull out the 3 objection patterns, draft new rebuttal scripts.

Basically: if it makes a business move forward and I don't have time to do it myself, it's on your plate. You'll operate across growth, product, hiring, and ops.

## Who you are:

- **AI-native.** You don't just "use ChatGPT" — you build with Cursor, Lovable, v0, Claude, whatever ships fastest. You have opinions on which tool for which job.
- **Can vibe-code.** You've built landing pages, simple tools, or MVPs. You don't need to be a full engineer, but you can read code, ship features, and debug with AI assistance.
- **Paid-ads literate.** You've run Meta (or TikTok / Google) ads yourself, not just read Twitter threads about them. You understand angles, hooks, CTR, CAC.
- **Ridiculously fast learner.** The specific tasks will change every week. Your job is to be the person who figures new things out quickly.
- **Bias to action.** You'd rather ship something rough today than something perfect next week.
- **Great judgment.** You can tell when to DIY, when to delegate, when to ask. High trust, low drama. I'll be talking to you every day. We need to vibe.

## Bonus points:

- You've built something of your own (an app, a newsletter, a side business).
- You've worked at an early-stage start-up or agency where you wore many hats.
- You have a strong point of view on growth / product / design.

## Not for you if:

- You need a detailed job description to know what to do next.
- You want to specialize deeply in one function.
- You thrive on process and predictability.

## Compensation

Competitive base + performance upside. I'd rather pay top-of-market for one A-player than hire two decent people.

## How to apply

Send me:
1. One thing you've built, shipped, or scaled that you're proud of (link please, not a description).
2. A landing page, ad, or small tool built with AI tools in under a day, even a new one you'd make just for this application.
3. One business in my portfolio you've looked up, and one specific thing you'd do in your first week.

No cover letters. No resumes as the first contact. Just show me.

## What this JD tells us about Max (mine for the Loom)

The JD is itself a positioning artifact. Max wrote this and approved it. Things to extract:

### He literally hires people who build with AI tools

> *"AI-native. You don't just 'use ChatGPT' — you build with Cursor, Lovable, v0, Claude, whatever ships fastest. You have opinions on which tool for which job."*

This single line completes the AI-reliance neutralization. Max is **explicitly screening FOR** AI-native builders. The Loom should call this out: *"You wrote in your JD that you want someone who builds with Cursor, Lovable, v0, Claude. Here's what that looks like."*

### "Vibe-code" is his term

Used twice. He has internalized the verb. The Loom can use it back at him without it feeling forced.

### Speed is the #1 trait

> *"Bias to action. You'd rather ship something rough today than something perfect next week."*

Translation: don't over-polish. Submit Day 1 over Day 2 if it's good enough. Show speed in how the submission opens.

### The Monday-Friday example IS the test

Look at what he listed for an actual week:
- **Monday:** Meta ads (this challenge has it)
- **Tuesday:** Vibe-coded landing page or onboarding (this challenge has it)
- **Wednesday:** Cold outreach to senior hires (this challenge has it)
- **Thursday:** Data audit + Loom (this challenge has the Loom)
- **Friday:** Sales call analysis (this isn't in the challenge)

The challenge is a 48-hour compressed version of his actual week. **Mon-Wed of his real job is exactly what we're building.**

### Format preferences

- *"No cover letters. No resumes as the first contact. Just show me."* → He hates fluff. The submission's opening line should *not* be "I'm excited to apply for this role."
- *"A landing page, ad, or small tool built with AI tools in under a day"* → Speed-built artifacts are his standard, not below it.

### The phrase "high trust, low drama"

Worth mirroring somewhere. It's how he describes his preferred working relationship.

### "We need to vibe"

He says this literally. Confidence and warmth matter alongside competence. The submission's tone should feel like talking to someone, not pitching at someone.
