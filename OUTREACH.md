# Snowball Head of Sales — outreach DMs

> The first message I'd send each candidate. None of these lead with comp. Each leads with a specific receipt from their own work or a specific signal in their bio. Channel is calibrated per candidate (LinkedIn vs X) — operators reply to the platform they live on, not the one a recruiter happens to find them through.
>
> For the framework that produced these candidates, see [CANDIDATE_SHORTLIST.md](CANDIDATE_SHORTLIST.md). For full per-candidate context (why each, what they'd say no to, how they'd actually take the role), see [research/08_candidate_dossiers.md](research/08_candidate_dossiers.md).

The "Pier Valley tells me you already value the autonomy part" line shows we read past the headline role and into his independent consulting practice, which signals real research rather than recruiter-form-letter. No comp mention; the conversation about money belongs to call-1.

## What I'd track after sending

| Metric | Indicator |
|---|---|
| **Open / read receipt** | LinkedIn shows read time. X DM shows seen status. |
| **Reply within 48 hrs** | Highest signal of interest |
| **Reply within 7 days** | Mid signal — schedule call |
| **No reply at 7 days** | Send a single, short follow-up. One. Not three. |
| **No reply at 14 days** | Move on. They saw it; they passed. |

The follow-up at day 7, if needed: *"Realize this is in your inbox. No worries either way — happy to talk if it's still interesting."* Eight words can revive a thread that twelve can't.
